DRONE-ROBOT
(DROT)

The drone that picks the fruit your crew can't reach in time.

DROT is a revolutionary harvesting drone built for hard-fruit growers. Every harvest is a race against a narrow window: when an orchard ripens, the whole crop comes due within a few days — but there are rarely enough hands to bring it all in. Between labor shortages and harvest windows that close in just two or three days, growers routinely leave 20–30% of their fruit unpicked on the tree, where it drops, rots, and turns into lost revenue. DROT closes that gap, recovering the fruit that would otherwise go to waste.

The Problem

The problem, stated simply.

When the fruit is ready, the clock is already running — and most farms can't beat it.

01 — Labor

Not enough hands

Labor shortages mean there aren't enough pickers when the fruit is ready — and the cost of labor, when it can be found, is high.

02 — Time

The window slams shut

A large orchard ripens faster than a crew can harvest it — often the entire crop within two to three days.

03 — Waste

Fruit left to rot

The result: 20–30% of the fruit is left on the tree, unharvested and lost.

Mission

Where we are.
Where we're going.

Today

Recover the harvest

Our mission is to give growers a faster, more cost-effective way to harvest — one that reduces waste, recovers the fruit that would otherwise be left on the tree, and turns lost crops back into revenue.

Tomorrow

A drone-robot hybrid

DRONE-ROBOT (DROT) is only the beginning. Our long-term mission is to create a versatile drone-robot hybrid: a platform that combines flight with the ability to grab, lift, and manipulate objects in places today's robots simply can't reach. Beyond the orchard, that means structural inspections, retrieval from heights, and work in environments that are dangerous or inaccessible for people and ground-based machines alike.
